pContest Rules

In order to participate in the photography constest, the user must accept the following rules:

  • pContest assumes that every user owns the rights of the picture
  • pContest accepts from fifteen to sixty participants
  • pContest would only keep personal data for as long as it is necessary as said in the Data Protection Act
  • pContest reserves the right to ban any account alias deemed offensive or against the rules
  • Our retention policy is three months after the contest closing
